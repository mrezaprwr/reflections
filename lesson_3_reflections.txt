What are some situations when branches would be helpful in keeping your history organized? How would branches help?

Branches would be helpful if you want to add another version of your file but not sure about that file yet. Branches help by making another version without actually 
change the real version of it.

What is the result of merging two branches together? Why do we represent it in the diagram the way we do?

The result of merging two branches is to update the master branch with other version of branch without actually deleting the remaining commit in other branches.
